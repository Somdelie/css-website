We will provide you the best work which you dreamt for!

Consolidated Steel Structures, a reputable steel framing construction company; has been in business since 2006. Our slogan, “Our Reputation Speaks for Itself” is indicative of the company success, and recent changes to our new company logo and slogan represents and reflects our philosophy of creating and designing innovative and modern steel structures.

One of the key competencies of CSS is that they will not simply design and construct your steel structure but a client centric approach is taken to maximize efficiency and minimize costs. CSS endeavours to find alternative designs to economize building designs for clients. Working closely with engineers to ensure the highest safety and quality levels are achieved.
